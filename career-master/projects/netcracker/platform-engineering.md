# Platform Engineering & Observability

**Company:** [Netcracker](../companies/netcracker.md)  
**Period:** July 2025 — Present  
**Role on project:** System Architect

## Problem

Enterprise ERP platform lacked standardized cloud-native deployment, centralized observability, and operational readiness for Kubernetes — with fragmented logging, metrics, and tracing across 100+ modules.

## Solution

- **Platform migration:** OpenShift → Kubernetes; Docker containerization; GitLab CI/CD pipelines
- **Java platform upgrade:** Java 8 → 17 with build modernization and runtime optimization
- **Observability:** OpenTelemetry instrumentation, OpenSearch (Elasticsearch migration), Grafana dashboards — integrated across ERP applications
- **Identity & security:** ADFS integration, Keycloak identity provider, RBAC, JWT, OPA policy standards
- **Infrastructure:** MinIO object storage, Vault secrets management
- **Operations:** Performance analysis, log aggregation, alerting, production readiness, regression and integration testing support
- **Enablement:** Kubernetes training for ERP teams; microservices and modernization workshops

## Impact

- Metrics integrated across all applications
- Centralized logging, metrics collection, and distributed tracing operational across ERP applications
- ADFS and Keycloak identity integration with RBAC, JWT, and OPA policy enforcement
- Cloud-native deployment patterns standardized across enterprise applications
- Presented Kubernetes enablement to ERP team; Best Presenter for microservices sessions
