# eTreasury — Universal Integrated Banking

**Company:** [TCS](../companies/tcs.md)  
**Period:** August 2007 — July 2008  
**Role on project:** Software Developer  
**Team size:** 40  
**Customers:** HDFC, RBI, Vijaya Bank, BOI, CBI, Mercantile Bank, BOM, and others

## Problem

Banks needed a universal integrated banking solution for foreign exchange and domestic treasury operations in multi-currency, multi-branch environments.

## Solution

- eTreasury product on PowerBuilder 6.5, C++, VC++
- UNIX Solaris platform with Tuxedo application server and Oracle 9i
- Screen design and subroutine development in PowerBuilder
- CMR (Change Management Request) development and bug fixing

## Impact
