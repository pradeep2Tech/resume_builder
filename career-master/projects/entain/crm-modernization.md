# CRM Modernization & Engineering Standardization

**Company:** [Entain (Ivy Comptech)](../companies/entain.md)  
**Period:** 2015 — 2025 (ongoing initiatives across tenure)  
**Role on project:** Software Architect / Principal Engineer

## Problem

Fragmented CRM and reward-related systems with inconsistent data models, duplicated engineering effort, and oversized teams (90+ engineers) maintaining legacy monoliths across gaming and payments platforms.

## Solution

- Standardized data models and CRM systems across customer engagement domains
- Migrated legacy monoliths to cloud-native microservices (Java, Golang)
- Streamlined engineering processes, HLD/LLD governance, and reusable API frameworks
- Unified reward and offer displays across payments and gaming platforms
- Centralized customer-centric system integrating campaigns and rewards
- Implemented observability and application telemetry with Elastic APM

## Impact

- Reduced workforce from **90+ to 40 engineers** while improving productivity by **50%+**
- **30%** latency reduction through architectural modernization
- Significant operational cost and maintenance overhead reduction
- Seamless user experience across payments and gaming platforms

## Tech stack

`Java` `Golang` `Spring Boot` `Redis` `CockroachDB` `Kafka` `Kubernetes` `Elastic APM`
