# Issue Analyzer

**Company:** [Envestnet Yodlee](../companies/envestnet-yodlee.md)  
**Period:** August 2013 — May 2014  
**Role on project:** Team Lead / Requirement Analyst  
**Team size:** 4  
**Client:** Yodlee — internal advisory application

## Problem

Customer service teams manually analyzed client issues, requiring significant manpower and slow resolution times for financial data aggregation support cases.

## Solution

- Designed application architecture from scratch with rule-based system and custom prediction algorithms
- Built internal advisory tool to analyze issues and recommend quick resolutions for CS teams
- Stakeholder requirement gathering, prioritization, and functional specification
- Unit testing and quality assurance for production reliability

## Impact

- Reduced manpower requirements by **7 engineering resources**
- Improved response time and accuracy for customer issue resolution
- Optimized service request efficiency for Customer Service teams

## Tech stack

`Java` `Spring MVC` `JBoss` `ANT` `XML` `Castor` `Custom prediction algorithms` `Rule engine`
